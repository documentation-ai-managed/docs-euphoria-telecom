---
title: Cannot Make Calls
description: Fix call connection issues on Euphoria Telecom.
---

## Issue

- Outgoing calls fail
- Error: "Call failed to connect"

## Causes

- Incorrect SIP credentials
- Network restrictions

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    ````
    Ensure your extension and password in your softphone match those in your Euphoria dashboard.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test network access">
    ````bash
    ping sip.euphoria.co.za
    ````
    Ensure ports 5060 (UDP/TCP) are open on your network.
  </Step>
</Steps>
