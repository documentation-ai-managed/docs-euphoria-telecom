---
title: Setting Up a Softphone
description: How to register a softphone with Euphoria Telecom.
---

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download a softphone app">
    ````
    E.g. Zoiper, Linphone, or Bria for your platform.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new SIP account">
    ````
    Username: [your extension number]
    Password: [your SIP password]
    Domain: sip.euphoria.co.za
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and register">
    ````
    The app should show 'Registered'. Place a test call to verify.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
