## Getting Started

1. Register an account at [portal.euphoria.co.za](https://portal.euphoria.co.za)
2. Activate your account through the confirmation email
3. Log in using your credentials
4. Access your dashboard to manage your services

## Need help?

Contact support@euphoria.co.za or visit our Community for assistance.
