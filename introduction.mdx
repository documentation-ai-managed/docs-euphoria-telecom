## Overview

Euphoria Telecom provides a flexible, contract-free VoIP phone system tailored for businesses and contact centers of all sizes. You gain access to advanced features like AI-driven call summaries, custom routing menus, real-time agent monitoring, and detailed analytics for call trends and customer experience. Deploy quickly without long-term commitments and scale as your needs evolve.

This documentation guides you through setup, configuration, and advanced usage to maximize your system's potential.
